Flight of the Hippogriff

The Flight of the Hippogriff is at Univershal studioes. The Flight of the Hippogriff is a fun, but short rollercoaster. This rollercoaster goes up and down and side to side, but does not have any loops. Where you will wait is Hagrid's hut. When you start going on the ride you will see a hippogriff from harry potter called Buckbeak, or later on the series Witherwings. Below this there will be a photo of the Flight of the Hippogriff.
